Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Pestify (Pest – Free) uses cookies and similar technologies on pestify.gr.

Effective Date: 23/02/2026  •  Last Updated: 23/02/2026

Pestify is operated by Pest – Free (Owner: Χρήστος Παμπόρης / Christos Pamporis), Greece. For general personal data processing information, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage (e.g., localStorage/sessionStorage) or technical identifiers used to enable basic site functions.

In the EU, the use of cookies is primarily regulated by the ePrivacy rules (implemented nationally) and complemented by GDPR requirements for transparency and lawful processing.

2. How pestify.gr uses cookies today

At the time of this policy, pestify.gr does not use analytics cookies or marketing/advertising cookies. We do not run behavioral tracking, profiling, or remarketing on this website.

2.1 Strictly necessary (technical) cookies

Our marketing website is informational and does not require accounts or logins. As a result, we do not intentionally set cookies for login sessions on pestify.gr.

However, basic technical operations by your browser and our hosting infrastructure (e.g., security protections, performance, or server functionality) may involve strictly necessary technical storage. Where such cookies exist, they are used only to provide the website you request and do not require consent under EU cookie rules.

2.2 Analytics cookies

We do not use analytics cookies on pestify.gr at this time (for example, Google Analytics).

2.3 Marketing cookies

We do not use marketing or advertising cookies on pestify.gr at this time (for example, Google Ads conversion tracking).

3. Calendly (demo booking)

If you request a demo through Calendly (for example, via a Calendly booking flow), Calendly may process your information and may use its own cookies or similar technologies. Calendly’s processing is governed by its own policies.

We recommend reviewing Calendly’s privacy/cookie information before submitting a booking. Pestify does not control cookies that may be set by Calendly.

4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies (where they exist) may affect basic website functionality.

Browser help pages typically explain how to manage cookies for your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).

5. Changes to this Cookie Policy

If we introduce analytics, advertising, or other non-essential cookies in the future, we will:

  • Update this Cookie Policy
  • Implement a consent mechanism where legally required
  • Provide the ability to withdraw consent
